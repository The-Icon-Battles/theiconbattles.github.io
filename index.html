Frequently Asked Questions

Does it read or write something to my files?
No, it only gets the positions, names, and icon images through Windows API but not content.
Why I can't see icons like "My PC", "Trash Bin" etc. in app?
"Special folders" are currently not supported in The Icon Battles due to the complexity of getting images of their icons. But it will be fixed in future updates.
